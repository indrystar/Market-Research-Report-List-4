<p><ul><li>One Sided</li><li>Double Sided</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aluminum foil shielding tape is available in two main types: one-sided and double-sided. One-sided tape has a layer of conductive adhesive on one side, making it ideal for applications where only one surface needs to be shielded. Double-sided tape, on the other hand, has conductive adhesive on both sides, providing excellent shielding for both surfaces it is applied to. Both types are commonly used in industries such as electronics, telecommunications, and aerospace for electromagnetic interference (EMI) shielding purposes.</p></p>
