<p><p>Nondestructive Testing (NDT) includes various methods such as Eddy Current Inspection, Magnetic Particle Testing, Electromagnetic Testing, Liquid Penetrant Testing, Radiography, and Ultrasonic Testing. Eddy Current Inspection uses electrical currents to detect surface and subsurface defects. Magnetic Particle Testing examines materials for surface cracks by applying a magnetic field. Electromagnetic Testing detects flaws by inducing electric currents in the material. Liquid Penetrant Testing identifies surface-breaking flaws using a dye. Radiography uses X-rays to inspect internal structures. Ultrasonic Testing sends high-frequency sound waves to detect defects within materials.</p></p>
