<p><p>Printed woven bags market consists of two main types, laminated and non-laminated woven bags. Laminated woven bags are coated with a layer of polypropylene to enhance durability and moisture resistance, making them suitable for packaging applications where extra protection is required. Non-laminated woven bags are uncoated and are commonly used for products that do not require as much protection, such as agriculture and construction materials. Both types offer customization options for printing logos, designs, and information for branding purposes.</p></p>
