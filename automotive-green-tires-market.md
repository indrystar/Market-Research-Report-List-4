<p><strong>The Automotive Green Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bias Tire</li><li>Radial Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive green tires market can be classified into two main types: bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires are constructed with multiple layers of rubber-coated fabric crisscrossed at different angles, providing stability and durability. Radial tires, on the other hand, have layers of fabric arranged at 90-degree angles from the direction of travel, offering better handling and fuel efficiency. Both types of tires are designed to reduce rolling resistance and improve fuel efficiency, making them eco-friendly options for vehicles.</p></p>
