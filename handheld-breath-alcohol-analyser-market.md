<p><strong>The Handheld Breath Alcohol Analyser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Sample</li><li>Passive Sample</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handheld breath alcohol analyzers in the market can be categorized into two main types: direct sample and passive sample. Direct sample analyzers require the user to blow into a tube or mouthpiece to provide a breath sample for analysis. Passive sample analyzers, on the other hand, can detect alcohol vapors in the surrounding air without the need for a breath sample from the user. Both types of analyzers are commonly used by law enforcement agencies, employers, and individuals to quickly and accurately assess blood alcohol levels.</p></p>
