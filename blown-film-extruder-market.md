<p><p>A blown film extruder is a type of plastic extrusion machine used to create thin plastic films. The process involves feeding plastic pellets into the extruder, which melts the material and then forces it through a circular die to form a continuous tube of plastic film. The tube is inflated with air to expand it to the desired thickness and width, then cooled and flattened to create a flat film.</p><p>The blown film extruder market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by the increasing demand for packaging materials in various industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and consumer goods. Blown film extruders offer benefits such as high production speed, flexibility in film thickness and width, and cost-effectiveness, making them a popular choice for manufacturing packaging films.</p><p>The latest trends in the blown film extruder market include the development of more energy-efficient and eco-friendly machines, as well as advancements in materials and processing techniques to improve film quality and performance.
