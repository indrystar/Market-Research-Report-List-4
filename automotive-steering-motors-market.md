<p><strong>The Automotive Steering Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric Power Steering (EPS)</li><li>Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive steering motors are classified into two main types: Electric Power Steering (EPS) and Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS). EPS systems use an electric motor to assist in steering, offering greater efficiency and responsiveness. On the other hand, EHPS systems combine hydraulic power with electronic control for steering assistance. Both types aim to improve steering performance and control in vehicles, with EPS being more commonly used in modern vehicles due to its energy efficiency and advanced technology.</p></p>
