<p><p>FISH testing probes are used in various markets such as the single color, dual color, and multi-color markets. In the single color market, probes target a single specific gene or chromosomal region. Dual color probes combine two different colored fluorophores to detect two different target regions simultaneously. Multi-color probes utilize three or more different fluorophores to detect multiple target regions at once. These different types of FISH testing probes cater to a wide range of research and diagnostic needs in various fields.</p></p>
