<p><p>Nitinol-based guide wires come in three main types - angled, J-shaped, and straight. Angled guide wires are designed to navigate around corners and obstacles within the body during medical procedures. J-shaped guide wires are specifically curved to access hard-to-reach areas or to provide additional support during surgeries. Straight guide wires are used for more straightforward procedures that require precise navigation through the body. Each type serves a specific purpose in guiding medical devices through the body with accuracy and efficiency.</p></p>
