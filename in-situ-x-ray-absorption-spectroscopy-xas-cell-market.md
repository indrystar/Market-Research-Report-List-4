<p><p>In-situ X-ray Absorption Spectroscopy (XAS) Cell Market offers two main types: Transmission (TM-XAS) and Fluorescent (FL-XAS). TM-XAS involves the direct measurement of X-ray transmission through the sample, providing information on the absorption characteristics. On the other hand, FL-XAS utilizes the fluorescent emission of the sample when exposed to X-rays, allowing for a more sensitive detection method. Both types of XAS cells are crucial in studying the electronic and structural properties of materials in real-time under various conditions.</p></p>
