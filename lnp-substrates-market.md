<p><p>Lnp substrates market consists of two main types: Semiconducting substrates and semi-insulating substrates. Semiconducting substrates are used in electronics industry for their conductive properties, allowing for the flow of electricity through the material. Semi-insulating substrates, on the other hand, have high resistance to electrical current, making them suitable for applications where electrical insulation is required. Both types of substrates play a key role in various industries such as semiconductor manufacturing, telecommunications, and optoelectronics.</p></p>
