<p><p>One piece adult colostomy bags are designed as a single unit with the adhesive wafer and pouch attached together. They are convenient and easy to use, making them a popular choice for many colostomy patients. On the other hand, two piece adult colostomy bags consist of a separate adhesive wafer and pouch that can be connected together. This design allows for easier customization and flexibility in changing the pouch without having to remove the entire unit from the skin.</p></p>
