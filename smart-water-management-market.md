<p><p>Smart water management includes two main market types - AMR (Automatic Meter Reading) Meters and AMI (Advanced Metering Infrastructure) Meters. AMR Meters are traditional water meters that collect consumption data remotely, while AMI Meters go a step further by providing real-time data and two-way communication between the meter and the utility company. Both types of meters help in improving water efficiency, detecting leaks, and reducing non-revenue water, ultimately leading to better overall water management and conservation efforts.</p></p>
