<p><p>Photo printing market can be broadly classified into two types: Film printing and Digital printing. Film printing involves developing photos captured on film rolls using a chemical process to create physical prints. On the other hand, digital printing involves printing photos directly from digital files onto paper or other materials using inkjet or laser printers. Both markets cater to different preferences and technologies, with film printing appealing to traditionalists and digital printing offering convenience and versatility.</p></p>
