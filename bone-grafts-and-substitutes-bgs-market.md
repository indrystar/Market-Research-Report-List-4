<p><p>Bone Grafts and Substitutes (BGS) market includes various types such as Ceramic-Based BGS, Polymer-Based BGS, Growth Factor-Based BGS, Cell-Based BGS, Harvested BGS, and Others. Ceramic-Based BGS utilize materials like calcium phosphate or calcium sulfate. Polymer-Based BGS use materials like polylactic acid or polyglycolic acid. Growth Factor-Based BGS involve the use of proteins to stimulate bone growth. Cell-Based BGS involve the use of stem cells to help with bone regeneration. Harvested BGS involve taking bone from the patient's own body. Others include materials like demineralized bone matrix or coralline hydroxyapatite.</p></p>
