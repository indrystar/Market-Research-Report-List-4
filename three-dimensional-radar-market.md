<p><p>Three-dimensional radar systems can be classified into three main categories based on their range capabilities: long range, medium range, and short range. </p><p>Long-range radar systems are capable of detecting and tracking targets at extended distances, typically over hundreds of kilometers. </p><p>Medium-range radar systems cover a moderate distance range, typically up to a few tens of kilometers, making them suitable for applications such as air traffic control and weather monitoring. </p><p>Short-range radar systems are designed for close-range detection, typically up to a few kilometers, and are commonly used for applications like collision avoidance in vehicles and surveillance in urban environments.</p></p>
