<p><ul><li>Single Roller Method</li><li>Double Roller Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Patterned glass is manufactured using different methods based on the desired patterns and textures. The Single Roller Method involves passing glass sheets through a single roller that imprints a specific pattern onto the surface. On the other hand, the Double Roller Method utilizes two rollers to create more intricate designs on the glass. Both methods cater to different market demands, with the Single Roller Method being more cost-effective for simpler patterns and the Double Roller Method offering a wider range of design options for high-end applications.</p></p>
