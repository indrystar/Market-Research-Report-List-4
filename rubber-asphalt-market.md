<p><p>Rubber Asphalt Market includes three types: SBR Modified Asphalt, Nitrile Rubber Modified Asphalt, and Natural Rubber Modified Asphalt. SBR Modified Asphalt is made by blending styrene-butadiene rubber with asphalt, providing improved durability and flexibility. Nitrile Rubber Modified Asphalt uses nitrile rubber to enhance resistance to fuels and oils, making it popular in road construction. Natural Rubber Modified Asphalt involves incorporating natural rubber into the asphalt mix, resulting in improved elasticity and resistance to cracking. Each type offers unique benefits for various applications in the construction industry.</p></p>
