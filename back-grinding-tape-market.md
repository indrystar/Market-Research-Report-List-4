<p><p>Back Grinding Tape Market is segmented into UV Curable and Non-UV Curable types. UV Curable tapes use ultraviolet light to cure the adhesive, providing fast adhesion and high bond strength. On the other hand, Non-UV Curable tapes do not require UV light for curing and offer good adhesion properties at room temperature. Both types have their own advantages and are used based on the specific requirements of the application. UV Curable tapes are preferred for applications requiring quick curing and high bond strength, while Non-UV Curable tapes are suitable for standard applications.</p></p>
