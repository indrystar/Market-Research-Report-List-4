<p><p>Nitro Cellulose market can be divided into two primary types: Flocculent and Threadiness. Flocculent nitro cellulose forms a gel-like substance when mixed with water, and is primarily used in industries such as cosmetics and pharmaceuticals. Threadiness nitro cellulose, on the other hand, forms a thread-like texture when mixed with water, and is commonly used in industries like textiles and adhesives. Both types play a significant role in various sectors due to their unique properties and applications.</p></p>
