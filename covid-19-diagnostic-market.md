<p><p>The Covid-19 diagnostic market includes various types of testing methods such as immunoassays, genomic sequencing, and whole genome sequencing (WGS). Immunoassays detect antibodies produced in response to the virus, while genomic sequencing identifies specific viral genetic material. WGS provides a more detailed analysis of the virus's entire genetic makeup. Additionally, rRT-PCR (reverse transcription polymerase chain reaction) is a widely used method for detecting the presence of the virus by amplifying and analyzing viral RNA.</p></p>
