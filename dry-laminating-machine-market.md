<p><strong>The Dry Laminating Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coating Dry Laminating Machine</li><li>Pre-coated Dry Laminating Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dry laminating machines are used for applying a layer of adhesive to different substrates. There are two main types of dry laminating machines based on the type of coating used: coating dry laminating machine and pre-coated dry laminating machine. Coating dry laminating machines apply the adhesive directly onto the substrate during the laminating process. On the other hand, pre-coated dry laminating machines use pre-coated films or foils that are then laminated onto the substrate. Both types of machines are widely used in packaging and printing industries.</p></p>
