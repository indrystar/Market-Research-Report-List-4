Multi-point Sampling Systems have multiple sampling points for more comprehensive monitoring of contamination levels across a larger area, while Single-point Sampling Systems have one sampling point for smaller spaces or localized monitoring needs. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining cleanroom air quality and ensuring product integrity.</p></p>
