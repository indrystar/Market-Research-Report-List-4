<p><p>Advanced Harmonic Filters Market consists of three main types: Passive Filters, Active Filters, and Hybrid Filters. Passive Filters are simple, cost-effective solutions for reducing harmonics in electrical systems. Active Filters use electronic components to actively eliminate harmonics and can provide more precise filtering. Hybrid Filters combine elements of both passive and active filters, offering a balanced solution for filtering harmonics. Each type has its own advantages and is used depending on the application and specific needs of the electrical system.</p></p>
