<p><ul><li>Tack Applicators</li><li>Glue Applicators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical mesh fixator market includes tack applicators and glue applicators. Tack applicators are devices used to secure medical mesh in place during surgeries by placing small tacks or staples through the mesh and into surrounding tissues. Glue applicators, on the other hand, are used to apply adhesive to the mesh for fixation. Both types of fixators provide stability and support to the mesh implant, aiding in its successful integration with the surrounding tissues for optimal patient outcomes.</p></p>
