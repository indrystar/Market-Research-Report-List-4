<p><p>Contactless IC card chips come in two main types: single chip and dual chip. Single chip cards contain only one integrated circuit chip, which stores all data and handles communication with the card reader. Dual chip cards, on the other hand, have two separate chips for storing and processing data, providing additional security and functionality. Both types of cards are commonly used for various applications, such as transportation, payment, and access control systems.</p></p>
