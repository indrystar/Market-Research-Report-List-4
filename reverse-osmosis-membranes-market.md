<p><p>The reverse osmosis membranes market comprises three primary types: cellulose acetate membranes, aromatic hydrazide membranes, and aromatic polyamide membranes. Cellulose acetate membranes are known for their low fouling and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for various applications. Aromatic hydrazide membranes offer enhanced chemical resistance and durability, ideal for challenging conditions. Aromatic polyamide membranes are characterized by high rejection rates and thermal stability, making them popular in water purification and desalination processes. Each type caters to specific industrial needs and performance requirements.</p></p>
