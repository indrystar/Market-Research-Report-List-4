<p><p>Bluetooth trackers are essential for locating and managing personal items. The market includes Radio Frequency (RF) trackers, which use traditional radio waves for tracking, providing a longer range but typically requiring line-of-sight. In contrast, Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) tags are prevalent for their energy efficiency and compatibility with smartphones, enabling real-time tracking through mobile applications. BLE tags are ideal for short-range tracking, making them popular in various sectors, including retail, logistics, and personal use for efficient asset management.</p></p>
