<p><p>Desktop RFID and Barcode printers can be categorized into two market types: Smart and Standard. Smart printers are equipped with advanced features such as connectivity options like Wi-Fi and Bluetooth, as well as the ability to read and write RFID tags. They are ideal for industries requiring real-time tracking and inventory management. On the other hand, Standard printers are more basic, offering standard barcode printing functionalities without RFID capabilities. They are commonly used in retail, healthcare, and manufacturing industries for basic labeling and tracking needs.</p></p>
