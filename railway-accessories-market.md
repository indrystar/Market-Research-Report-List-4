Rail joints are used to connect two rail ends, while railway spikes are used to secure rails to the tie plates on the wooden or concrete sleepers. Tie plates distribute the weight of the train and secure the rails in place. Rail nuts and washers are used to fasten rail joints and plates. Other accessories in the market include fish plates, fish bolts, rail pads, and insulators for electric railways.</p></p>
