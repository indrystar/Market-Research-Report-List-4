<p><ul><li>Ethylene-based Elastomers</li><li>Propylene-based Elastomers</li><li>Styrene-block Copolymers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Elastic laminates market types include ethylene-based elastomers, propylene-based elastomers, and styrene-block copolymers. Ethylene-based elastomers are known for their flexibility and resilience, making them ideal for applications requiring stretchability. Propylene-based elastomers offer good haptics and sealing properties, making them suitable for use in hygiene products. Styrene-block copolymers are used for their excellent adhesion and compatibility with various substrates, making them a popular choice for elastic laminates in industries such as healthcare and personal care.</p></p>
