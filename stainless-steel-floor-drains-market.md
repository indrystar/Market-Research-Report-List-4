<p><p>Stainless steel floor drains come in various types to suit different needs. Traditional water seal floor drains use a water trap to prevent odors from escaping. Spring-type floor drains have a spring mechanism that opens and closes the drain. Suction stone floor drains use a stone cover to create suction and prevent water from backing up. Gravity floor drains rely on gravity to allow water to flow out. There are also other types of floor drains available in the market to cater to specific requirements.</p></p>
