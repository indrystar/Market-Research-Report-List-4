<p><strong>The PTFE Printed Circuit Boards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Layer</li><li>Multi-layer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PTFE printed circuit boards come in two main types: single layer and multi-layer. Single layer boards consist of a single layer of PTFE substrate with the conductor pattern printed on one side. They are ideal for simple designs and low-cost applications. On the other hand, multi-layer boards have multiple layers of PTFE substrate with conductive patterns on both sides. They are more complex and versatile, allowing for higher circuit density and better signal integrity. Both types cater to different design requirements and applications in the electronics industry.</p></p>
