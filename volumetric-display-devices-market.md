<p><strong>The Volumetric Display Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static-volume Display</li><li>Swept-volume Display</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Volumetric Display Devices use different techniques to create 3D images. Static-volume Display devices create 3D images without the need for moving parts, allowing for stable and continuous viewing. Swept-volume Display devices use rotating or moving mirrors to create 3D images by rapidly changing the displayed image from different perspectives. These two types of devices cater to different needs and preferences, with static-volume displays being more suitable for stationary viewing and swept-volume displays providing a dynamic viewing experience.</p></p>
