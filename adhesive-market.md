<p><strong>The Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-based</li><li>Solvent-based</li><li>Hot Melt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The adhesive market is categorized into three main types: water-based, solvent-based, and hot melt adhesives. Water-based adhesives utilize water as a primary solvent, making them environmentally friendly and easy to clean. Solvent-based adhesives contain organic solvents, providing strong bonding but presenting certain health and environmental concerns. Hot melt adhesives are thermoplastic materials that are solid at room temperature and melt upon heating, offering quick setting times and strong bonding for a variety of applications. Each type serves distinct purposes and industries.</p></p>
