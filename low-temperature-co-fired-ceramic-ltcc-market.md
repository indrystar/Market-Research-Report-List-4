<p><p>Low Temperature Co-fired Ceramic (LTCC) is a type of ceramic material that is used in electronics packaging and RF/microwave applications. LTCC technology allows for the integration of multiple components such as capacitors, resistors, and inductors onto a single substrate, making it ideal for miniaturizing electronic devices.</p><p>The LTCC market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.70% during the forecast period.
