<p><ul><li>Single Pump</li><li>Double Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The reusable blood hemodialyzer market can be classified into two types: single pump and double pump systems. Single pump hemodialyzers use one pump to circulate blood from the patient through the dialyzer and back, while double pump systems utilize two pumps for the same process. Single pump hemodialyzers are typically simpler and more cost-effective, while double pump systems offer more precise control over blood flow rates and are often used in more complex dialysis procedures. Both types aim to efficiently remove waste from the blood and maintain the patient's overall health.</p></p>
