<p><p>Self-regulating Thermostatic Radiator Valves are designed to regulate the flow of hot water through radiators in a heating system to maintain a consistent room temperature. Self-operate Thermostatic Radiator Valves utilize a wax-filled sensor to adjust the valve opening based on the temperature in the room. Electronic Thermostatic Radiator Valves, on the other hand, use electronic sensors and controls to provide more precise temperature regulation and can be controlled remotely through a smart device. Both types of valves offer efficient and customizable heating solutions for residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
