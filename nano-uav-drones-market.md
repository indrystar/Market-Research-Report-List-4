Fixed Wing drones have a wing structure similar to traditional airplanes and offer longer flight times and greater stability. Rotary Wing drones, on the other hand, have rotating blades that enable vertical takeoff and landing, making them more maneuverable in confined spaces. The "Others" category includes hybrid drones or unconventional designs that do not fall into the fixed or rotary wing categories.</p></p>
