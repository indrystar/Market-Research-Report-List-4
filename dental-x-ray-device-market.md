<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Dental X ray Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intraoral X-Ray Imaging</li><li>Extraoral X-Ray Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental X-ray devices are classified into two main types: Intraoral X-Ray Imaging and Extraoral X-Ray Imaging. Intraoral X-Ray Imaging involves taking X-ray images inside the mouth and includes bite-wing, periapical, and occlusal X-rays. These are commonly used for detecting cavities, monitoring tooth development, and assessing root health. Extraoral X-Ray Imaging, on the other hand, involves taking X-rays outside the mouth and includes panoramic, cephalometric, and cone-beam computed tomography (CBCT) scans. These are used for assessing overall dental and facial structures, as well as for planning orthodontic treatment.</p></p>
