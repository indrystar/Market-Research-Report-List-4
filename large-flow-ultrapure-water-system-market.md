<p><p>The Large Flow Ultrapure Water System market includes two main types: Reverse Osmosis+Ion Exchange and Reverse Osmosis+EDI. Reverse Osmosis+Ion Exchange systems combine the process of removing ions and impurities through reverse osmosis with the ion exchange process for producing ultrapure water. On the other hand, Reverse Osmosis+EDI systems use reverse osmosis to remove impurities and then pass the water through an electrodeionization process to further purify it. Both types of systems are used in industries requiring high-quality ultrapure water for various applications.</p></p>
