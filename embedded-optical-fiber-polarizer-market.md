<p><ul><li>SM Type</li><li>PM Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Embedded Optical Fiber Polarizer market consists of two main types - SM Type and PM Type. SM Type polarizers are single-mode fibers that are designed to filter out a specific polarization state of light, while PM Type polarizers are polarization-maintaining fibers that maintain the polarization state of light along the length of the fiber. Both types of polarizers are essential components in optical communication systems, helping to control and manipulate the polarization of light for various applications.</p></p>
