<p><p>Bed monitoring systems are devices used to monitor patient movement and vital signs while they are in their bed. There are two main types of bed monitoring systems - wireless and wired. Wireless bed monitoring systems use sensors and transmitters to send data wirelessly to a central monitoring system, providing real-time updates on patient status. Both types of systems are crucial in healthcare settings to ensure the safety and well-being of patients.</p></p>
