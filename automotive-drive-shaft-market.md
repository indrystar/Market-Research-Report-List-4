<p><p>Automotive drive shaft market consists of two main types: half shaft and propeller shaft. Half shafts are used in vehicles with front-wheel drive, connecting the transmission to the front wheels. Propeller shafts, on the other hand, are used in rear-wheel or four-wheel drive vehicles, linking the transmission to the rear differential. Both types play a crucial role in transferring power from the transmission to the wheels, enabling the vehicle to move and operate efficiently. Each type caters to specific vehicle configurations and driving conditions.</p></p>
