<p><ul><li>Custom Forging</li><li>Captive Forging</li><li>Catalog Forging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive closed die forgings are categorized into three main types: Custom Forging, Captive Forging, and Catalog Forging. Custom Forging involves the production of unique, tailor-made parts according to specific customer requirements. Captive Forging refers to the in-house production of forgings by automotive manufacturers for their own use. Catalog Forging involves the manufacturing of standard forgings that are readily available for purchase by various automotive companies. Each of these market types caters to different needs within the automotive industry, providing customized solutions, in-house production capabilities, and standardized parts.</p></p>
