<p><p>Non-invasive ventilation machines are used to help individuals with respiratory issues breathe more easily. Two common types of non-invasive ventilation machines are CPAP (Continuous Positive Airway Pressure) and BiPAP (Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure) machines. CPAP machines deliver a constant level of air pressure, while BiPAP machines deliver varying levels of air pressure depending on the individual's breathing patterns. Both machines are used to treat conditions such as sleep apnea, COPD, and respiratory failure without invasive procedures like intubation.</p></p>
