<p><p>Hydraulic cementitious materials are categorized into three main types: Portland cement, Aluminate cement, and Sulfate cement. Portland cement is the most commonly used type and is known for its versatility and durability. Aluminate cement is a fast-setting material that is often used in construction projects requiring quick turnaround times. Sulfate cement is resistant to chemical attacks and is commonly used in projects where exposure to acidic or sulfate-rich environments is a concern. Each type of cementitious material offers unique properties and benefits for different construction applications.</p></p>
