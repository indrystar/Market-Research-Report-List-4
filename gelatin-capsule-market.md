<p><p>Gelatin capsules are available in various types depending on their solubility properties in the stomach or intestines. Stomach-soluble gelatin capsules dissolve in the stomach for immediate release of the contents. Enterosoluble vacant gelatin capsules dissolve in the intestines to protect sensitive ingredients from stomach acid. Other markets include specialized gelatin capsules for specific purposes such as controlled release, extended release, or enteric coating. These different types cater to the varying needs of the pharmaceutical and nutraceutical industries for effective delivery of ingredients to the body.</p></p>
