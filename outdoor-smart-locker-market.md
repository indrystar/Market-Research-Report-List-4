<p><p>Outdoor Smart Lockers can be classified into two main types: Contactless and Contact markets. Contactless smart lockers are operated through advanced technologies such as RFID and Bluetooth, allowing users to access their lockers without physical contact. On the other hand, Contact smart lockers require users to physically touch the locker for access, typically through keypads or biometric scanners. Both types offer secure and convenient storage solutions, with Contactless lockers being more popular due to their hygienic and efficient operation.</p></p>
