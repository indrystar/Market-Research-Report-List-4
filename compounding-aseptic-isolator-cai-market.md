<p><p>Compounding Aseptic Isolators (CAI) are specialized containment systems used in pharmaceutical and biotech industries to handle sterile products. The two main types of CAIs are Sterility Test Isolators, used for sterility testing of pharmaceutical products, and Sterility Production Isolators, utilized in the aseptic manufacturing process. These isolators provide a controlled environment that prevents contamination, ensuring the sterility and safety of the products being handled. Sterility Test Isolators are specifically designed for conducting tests to determine the sterility of pharmaceutical products, while Sterility Production Isolators are used in the production of sterile products.</p></p>
