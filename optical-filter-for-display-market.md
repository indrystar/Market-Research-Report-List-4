Polycarbonate filters are lightweight and shatter-resistant, suitable for applications requiring impact resistance. Acrylic filters provide good optical properties at a lower cost compared to glass filters. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on factors like performance requirements, budget, and specific application needs in the display market.</p></p>
