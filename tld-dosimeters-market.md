<p><p>TLD dosimeters, or thermoluminescent dosimeters, are categorized by their sensitivity levels, primarily including 1 μSv and 200 μSv sensitivity types. The 1 μSv sensitivity dosimeters are used for precise measurements in low radiation environments, making them suitable for sensitive applications such as medical or research settings. In contrast, 200 μSv sensitivity dosimeters are employed in more general occupational settings, where higher radiation exposure may occur.
