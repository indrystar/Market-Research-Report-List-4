<p><p>Residential LPG water heaters are available in two main types - tankless LPG water heaters and tank LPG water heaters. Tankless LPG water heaters heat water on-demand, providing continuous hot water and saving energy by eliminating the need to constantly heat a tank of water. Tank LPG water heaters store hot water in a tank, providing a supply of hot water ready for use. Both types offer efficient heating solutions for residential properties, catering to different preferences and requirements.</p></p>
