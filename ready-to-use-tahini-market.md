<p><p>Ready to use tahini is available in two main types: Hulled tahini and Unhulled tahini. Hulled tahini is made from sesame seeds with the outer shell removed, resulting in a smoother and milder flavor. On the other hand, Unhulled tahini is made from sesame seeds with the outer shell intact, giving it a stronger, more robust flavor. Both types of tahini can be used in a variety of dishes such as hummus, dressings, and sauces, depending on personal preference for taste and texture.</p></p>
