<p><p>The iron oxide pigments market is divided into two main types: natural and synthetic. Natural iron oxide pigments are derived from earthy minerals through mining and processing, resulting in eco-friendly colors used in paints, coatings, and construction materials. Synthetic iron oxide pigments, on the other hand, are chemically manufactured to achieve vibrant colors and superior properties, often used in plastics, cosmetics, and digital printing. Both types offer unique advantages, catering to diverse industrial applications and consumer preferences.</p></p>
