<p><strong>The Non-Contact Profilometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>White Light Interference</li><li>Confocal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-contact profilometers utilize different technologies for surface measurement, including White Light Interference and Confocal types. White Light Interference profilometers use multiple wavelengths of light to achieve high precision measurements, while Confocal profilometers use a pinhole aperture to eliminate out-of-focus light, providing accurate depth measurement. Both types offer non-contact surface profiling capabilities for applications requiring high resolution and precision, such as semiconductor manufacturing, medical devices, and automotive industries. These technologies enhance quality control processes by providing detailed, 3D surface topography data without physically touching the surface.</p></p>
