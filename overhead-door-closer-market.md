<p><p>Overhead door closers come in two main types: single action and double action. Single action closers operate in one direction, typically closing the door automatically. Double action closers, on the other hand, operate in both directions, allowing the door to close automatically from either side. Each type has its own benefits and is designed to suit different types of doors and entrances. Single action closers are commonly used for interior doors, while double action closers are often preferred for heavy-duty or commercial applications.</p></p>
