<p><strong>The Metallic Foil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Stamping Foil</li><li>Cold Stamping Foil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metallic foil market comprises primarily two types: hot stamping foil and cold stamping foil. Hot stamping foil involves heating the foil and applying it to a substrate using pressure, resulting in vibrant and reflective designs. In contrast, cold stamping foil is applied at room temperature using adhesive, offering a more versatile and cost-effective option for intricate designs. Both types are widely used in packaging, labels, and graphic arts, enhancing visual appeal and brand identity through metallic finishes.</p></p>
