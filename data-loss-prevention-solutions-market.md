<p><p>Data Loss Prevention (DLP) solutions are categorized into three main market types: Network DLP, Storage DLP, and Endpoint DLP. Network DLP focuses on monitoring and controlling data as it moves across a network. Storage DLP is concerned with protecting data at rest in storage systems, including databases and file servers. Endpoint DLP, on the other hand, focuses on securing data on individual devices such as laptops, smartphones, and tablets. Each market type plays a critical role in preventing unauthorized access and leakage of sensitive information.</p></p>
