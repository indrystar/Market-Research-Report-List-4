<p><p>Architectural white marble can be divided into two main types: natural marble and artificial marble. Natural marble is sourced from quarries and is known for its unique veining and patterns. It is considered more luxurious and high-end, making it popular in upscale architectural projects. On the other hand, artificial marble is a man-made material produced from a combination of marble dust, resin, and pigments. It is more affordable and easier to maintain, making it a common choice for commercial and residential applications.</p></p>
