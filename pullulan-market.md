<p><p>Pullulan is a polysaccharide produced through the fermentation of starch by the fungus Aureobasidium pullulans. Its biodegradable nature positions it as a sustainable alternative to traditional plastic materials, which has boosted its popularity in recent years.</p><p>The Pullulan Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.59% during the forecast period.
