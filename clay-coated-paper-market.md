<p><ul><li>Round Clay</li><li>Flat Clay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Clay coated paper is classified into two main types: round clay and flat clay. Round clay coated paper features a smoother surface and higher gloss due to the clay being applied in a rounded particle form. This type is typically used for high-quality printing applications such as magazines and catalogs. On the other hand, flat clay coated paper has a less smooth surface and lower gloss, making it suitable for products like brochures and flyers. Both types offer varying levels of print quality and finish, catering to different market needs.</p></p>
