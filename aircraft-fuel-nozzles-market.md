<p><p>Aircraft fuel nozzles are available in various types to suit different aviation requirements. Centrifugal nozzles are designed to provide a high flow rate with minimal pressure drop. Evaporator tube nozzles are used for fuel atomization and combustion efficiency. Oil throwing nozzles are specialized for oil injection in gas turbines. Other types of fuel nozzles include thermal spray nozzles, sonic nozzles, and swirl nozzles, each catering to specific aircraft engine designs and performance needs. These different market types offer a range of options for aircraft fuel delivery systems.</p></p>
