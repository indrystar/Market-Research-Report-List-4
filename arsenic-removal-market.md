<p><p>Arsenic removal market offers various types of processes to remove arsenic from water sources. The precipitative process involves adding chemicals to water to form solid arsenic compounds. The adsorptive process utilizes materials that attract and bind arsenic molecules. Ion exchange process replaces arsenic ions with non-toxic ions. Membrane process physically separates arsenic compounds from water. Other methods include oxidation, coagulation, and filtration. These different processes provide solutions for varying levels of arsenic contamination in water sources.</p></p>
