<p><p>The wine cork stoppers market can be categorized into three types: natural cork stoppers, agglomerated cork stoppers, and capsulated cork stoppers. Natural cork stoppers are made from a single piece of natural cork bark, while agglomerated cork stoppers are composed of small pieces of cork bound together. Capsulated cork stoppers have a natural cork base with a protective outer layer. Each type offers different qualities and price points, catering to the diverse preferences of wine producers and consumers.</p></p>
