<p><strong>The Professional Microphone Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dynamic</li><li>Condenser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dynamic and condenser are the two main types of professional microphones in the market. Dynamic microphones are sturdy and versatile, making them ideal for live performances and recording loud sound sources. They are more affordable and do not require external power sources. On the other hand, condenser microphones are more sensitive and offer higher sound quality, making them suitable for recording studios and capturing subtle nuances in vocals and instruments. However, they are usually more expensive and require phantom power to operate.</p></p>
