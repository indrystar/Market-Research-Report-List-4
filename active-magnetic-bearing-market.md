<p><p>Active Magnetic Bearing (AMB) market can be classified into two types based on control systems - Analog Control and Digital Control. Analog Control systems use analog signals for controlling and adjusting the magnetic field in the bearing, while Digital Control systems utilize digital signals for the same purpose. Analog Control systems are simpler and less expensive, but they lack the precision and efficiency of Digital Control systems, which offer higher levels of accuracy and performance due to their ability to process digital signals more effectively.</p></p>
