<p><p>Ossicular Replacement Prostheses (ORP) are used to treat hearing loss caused by damage to the ossicles in the middle ear. There are two main types of ORP on the market: Partial Ossicular Replacement Prostheses (PORP) and Total Ossicular Replacement Prostheses (TORP). PORPs replace only one or two ossicles, while TORPs replace all three ossicles. Each type of ORP has its own set of benefits and drawbacks, and the choice between PORP and TORP depends on the individual patient's needs and circumstances.</p></p>
