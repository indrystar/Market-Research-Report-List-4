<p><p>Gusseted poly mailers come in two main types: side gusseted and bottom gusseted. Side gusseted mailers have extra material on the sides that allow for more capacity and flexibility when packing items. Bottom gusseted mailers have additional material at the bottom, providing more space and improving stability for bulkier items. Both types are commonly used for shipping and packaging items that require extra space or protection during transit. These mailers cater to different needs based on the shape and size of the items being packaged.</p></p>
