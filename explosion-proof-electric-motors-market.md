<p><p>Explosion-proof electric motors are categorized into different types based on their level of protection. The EXd Type motors are designed to contain and withstand an internal explosion. Increased-Safety Type motors have additional protective features to prevent sparks and excessive temperatures. Other types of explosion-proof motors include encapsulation or hermetically sealed designs to prevent any flammable gases or vapors from entering the motor. These classifications ensure that electric motors can be safely used in hazardous environments where the risk of explosions is high.</p></p>
