<p><p>Lined steel pipes are available in various types to suit different industrial applications. PP lined pipes are lined with polypropylene for chemical resistance. PTFE lined pipes have a lining of polytetrafluoroethylene for high corrosion resistance. PVDF lined pipes are lined with polyvinylidene fluoride for exceptional chemical resistance. Other types of lined steel pipes include those lined with materials like PVC or HDPE. Each type offers specific benefits and is used in industries where resistance to corrosive materials is essential.</p></p>
