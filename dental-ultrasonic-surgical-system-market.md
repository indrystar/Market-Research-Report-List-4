<p><p>Dental Ultrasonic Surgical Systems come in two types: High Frequency and Low Frequency. High Frequency systems operate at a frequency above 20 kHz, providing fast and precise cutting capabilities. They are ideal for delicate procedures requiring intricate incisions. On the other hand, Low Frequency systems operate at a frequency below 20 kHz, offering more power and less tissue damage during surgeries that require more force. Each type caters to different surgical needs based on the frequency of operation for optimal performance.</p></p>
