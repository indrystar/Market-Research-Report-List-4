<p><ul><li>Permanent IVC Filters</li><li>Retrievable IVC Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nitinol based filters are used in the medical industry for various applications, including Permanent IVC Filters and Retrievable IVC Filters. Permanent IVC Filters are designed to stay in the body long-term to prevent blood clots from reaching the lungs. Retrievable IVC Filters are temporary filters that can be removed once the risk of blood clots has passed. Both types of filters are made from Nitinol, a shape-memory alloy known for its flexibility and durability in medical devices.</p></p>
