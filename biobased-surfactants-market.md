<p><p>Biobased surfactants are categorized into four types based on their chemical structure and functionality - anionic, cationic, nonionic, and amphoteric. Anionic surfactants have a negatively charged head group, cationic surfactants have a positively charged head group, nonionic surfactants have no charged head group, and amphoteric surfactants can carry both positive and negative charges. Each type offers unique properties and benefits for various applications in industries such as personal care, household cleaning, agriculture, and industrial processes.</p></p>
