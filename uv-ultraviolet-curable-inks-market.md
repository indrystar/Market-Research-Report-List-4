<p><p>UV curable inks are used in various industries for printing applications. There are four main types of UV curable inks in the market: Free Radical Polymerization Type, Free Radical Addition Molding, Cationic Polymerization, and Acid Cure Type. The Free Radical Polymerization Type uses a polymerization reaction to cure the ink, while the Free Radical Addition Molding type involves a free radical reaction for curing. Cationic Polymerization uses cationic initiators for curing, and Acid Cure Type utilizes acid to cure the ink. Each type has its unique characteristics and applications in the market.</p></p>
