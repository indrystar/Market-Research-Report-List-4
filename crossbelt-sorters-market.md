<p><p>Linear cross belt sorting and ring cross belt sorting are two types of cross belt sorters used in distribution centers and warehouses. Linear cross belt sorting involves a straight conveyor system where items are sorted and redirected to different destinations. Ring cross belt sorting utilizes a circular conveyor system, allowing items to be sorted in a continuous loop before being directed to their respective destinations. Both types of cross belt sorters are efficient solutions for high-volume sorting processes, improving productivity and accuracy in material handling operations.</p></p>
