<p><p>Hydraulic brake fluid comes in different types based on their chemical compositions and properties. DOT 3, DOT 4, DOT 5, and DOT 5.1 are the most common types in the market. DOT 3 and DOT 4 are glycol-based fluids suitable for most vehicles, with DOT 4 offering higher boiling points. DOT 5 is silicone-based and not compatible with other types, while DOT 5.1 is a glycol-based fluid with higher heat resistance than DOT 4. Each type is designed for specific performance and compatibility requirements in brake systems.</p></p>
