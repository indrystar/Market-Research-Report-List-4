<p><ul><li>Lever Riveter</li><li>Manually Activated Rivet Gun</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hand riveting tools are commonly categorized into two main types: lever riveters and manually activated rivet guns. Lever riveters feature a lever that is pulled to compress and set the rivet, offering greater ease and efficiency in the riveting process. On the other hand, manually activated rivet guns require direct pressure from the user to compress and set the rivet. Both types of tools are commonly used in various industries for fastening and joining materials together securely.</p></p>
