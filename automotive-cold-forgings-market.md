<p><p>Automotive cold forgings market can be categorized into three types- Custom Forging, Captive Forging, and Catalog Forging. Custom Forging involves creating unique forged components based on specific requirements of individual customers. Captive Forging refers to in-house forging operations by automotive manufacturers for their own production needs. Catalog Forging involves producing standard forgings that are readily available for purchase by multiple customers. Each type serves different market needs and offers distinct advantages in terms of customization, cost-effectiveness, and availability.</p></p>
