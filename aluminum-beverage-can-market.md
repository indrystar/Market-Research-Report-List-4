<p><p>Aluminum beverage cans are commonly found in two main market types: pull-tab and stay-on-tab. Pull-tab cans feature a detachable metal tab that is pulled to open the can, while stay-on-tab cans have a tab that remains attached to the can after opening. Pull-tab cans were popular in the past but have largely been replaced by stay-on-tab cans due to safety and environmental concerns. Stay-on-tab cans are more secure and prevent littering from detached tabs, making them the preferred choice in the market today.</p></p>
