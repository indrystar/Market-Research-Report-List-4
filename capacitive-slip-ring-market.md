<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Multiple Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Capacitive slip rings are available in two main market types - Single Channel and Multiple Channel. Single channel slip rings are designed to transmit signals or power through a single path, making them ideal for applications where only one type of data or power source needs to be transferred. On the other hand, Multiple Channel slip rings have multiple independent pathways, allowing for the simultaneous transmission of various signals or power sources. This versatility makes them well-suited for more complex applications that require the transfer of multiple types of data or power.</p></p>
