<p><p>Payment Card Personalization Services market consists of three main types: Dye Sub, Retransfer, and Inkjet. Dye Sub technology involves printing directly onto the card, resulting in vibrant colors and high-quality images. Retransfer technology transfers the printed image onto a thin film before applying it to the card, ensuring precise alignment and durability. Inkjet technology, on the other hand, uses ink droplets to create detailed designs quickly and efficiently. Each market type offers unique advantages catered to different card personalization needs.</p></p>
