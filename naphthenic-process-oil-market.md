<p><p>Naphthenic process oils are classified into different market types based on their chemical structure and properties. Cyclopentane is a type of naphthenic process oil that contains five carbon atoms in a cyclic structure, offering good solvency and low volatility. Cyclohexane, on the other hand, contains six carbon atoms in a cyclic structure, providing better thermal stability and resistance to oxidation. Both of these market types are widely used in various industrial applications such as rubber processing, adhesives, and coatings due to their unique characteristics.</p></p>
