<p><ul><li>Antibody Tests</li><li>RNA (viral load) Test</li><li>Antibody-antigen Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Human Immunodeficiency Virus (HIV) testing market includes various types of tests such as antibody tests, RNA (viral load) tests, and antibody-antigen tests. Antibody tests detect the presence of antibodies produced by the immune system in response to HIV infection. RNA tests measure the amount of virus in the blood, known as viral load. Antibody-antigen tests detect both antibodies and antigens of the virus. Each type of test plays a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring HIV infection in individuals.</p></p>
