<p><ul><li>PFAS</li><li>PFPE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor coolant market types include PFAS (perfluoroalkyl substances) and PFPE (perfluoropolyether). PFAS coolants are synthetic chemicals known for their resistance to heat and chemicals, making them ideal for high-temperature applications in the semiconductor industry. PFPE coolants, on the other hand, are fluorinated oils with low volatility and high thermal stability, making them suitable for extreme temperature environments. Both types of coolants play a crucial role in maintaining optimal operating temperatures in semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
