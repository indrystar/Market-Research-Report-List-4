<p><p>Brain Computer Interface (BCI) technology market consists of two main types: Invasive BCI and Non-Invasive BCI. Invasive BCI involves the implantation of electrodes directly into the brain tissue, providing high quality signal acquisition but with potential risks such as infection. On the other hand, Non-Invasive BCI utilizes external sensors placed on the scalp to detect brain activity, making it a safer option but with lower signal quality. Both types have their unique advantages and limitations, catering to different applications and user needs in the market.</p></p>
