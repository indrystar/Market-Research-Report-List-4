<p><p>Portable Turbidity Analyzers are used to measure the cloudiness of a liquid, providing crucial information in various industries. The two main types are Scattered Light Turbidimeters and Transmitted Light Turbidimeters. Scattered Light Turbidimeters analyze the light scattered by particles in the liquid, while Transmitted Light Turbidimeters measure the light transmitted through the liquid. Both types have their own advantages and are used in different applications, such as water treatment plants, environmental monitoring, and industrial processes.</p></p>
