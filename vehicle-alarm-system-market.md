<p><p>The vehicle alarm system market encompasses various security technologies designed to protect vehicles from theft and unauthorized access. Central locking systems secure all doors with one command. Biometric capture devices utilize unique physical features for access control. Remote keyless entry allows users to unlock or start vehicles without physical keys. Steering locks immobilize the steering wheel to deter theft, while alarms warn of intrusions. Immobilizers prevent engine starting without the correct key, significantly enhancing vehicle security.</p></p>
