<p><p>Diagnostic & Medical Imaging market consists of various types such as X-ray Imaging Systems, Computed Tomography (CT) Scanners, Ultrasound Systems, Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) Systems, Nuclear Imaging Systems, and others. X-ray imaging systems use electromagnetic radiation to produce images of the internal structures of the body. CT scanners use computer-processed X-rays to create detailed cross-sectional images of the body. Ultrasound systems use sound waves to produce images of the body's organs and tissues. MRI systems use magnetic fields and radio waves to generate detailed images of the body's internal structures. Nuclear imaging systems use radioactive tracers to produce images of organs and tissues. Other types of imaging technologies may include positron emission tomography (PET) scanners, mammography systems, and fluoroscopy systems.</p></p>
