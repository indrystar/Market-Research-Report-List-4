<p><strong>The HPMC Capsule Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HPMC with gelling agent</li><li>HPMC without gelling agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HPMC capsules with gelling agent are used for formulations requiring controlled release or modified release of the active ingredient. These capsules provide a barrier that slows down the drug release, making them suitable for extended release medications. On the other hand, HPMC capsules without gelling agent are used for immediate release formulations where rapid dissolution and absorption of the drug are required. These capsules disintegrate quickly in the gastrointestinal tract, allowing for fast release of the active ingredient. Both types cater to different requirements in the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
