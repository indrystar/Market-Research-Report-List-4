<p><p>Seismic perimeter intrusion detection systems consist of three main components - seismic sensors, seismic servers, and seismic receivers. Seismic sensors are devices that detect vibrations or movements in the ground caused by potential intruders. Seismic servers process and analyze the data collected by the sensors to determine if there is a threat. Seismic receivers receive alerts from the servers and notify security personnel of any potential breaches in the perimeter. These components work together to provide effective security and protection for various facilities and properties.</p></p>
