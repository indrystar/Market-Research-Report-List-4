<p><p>The PVC insulated cable market is segmented into two main types: PVC sheathed armoured cables and PVC sheathed unarmoured cables. PVC sheathed armoured cables are designed with a protective metal layer, offering enhanced durability and resistance to physical damage, making them ideal for industrial applications. In contrast, PVC sheathed unarmoured cables lack this additional layer, making them more suitable for residential and light commercial uses where flexibility and ease of installation are prioritized. Both types are essential for electrical wiring solutions.</p></p>
