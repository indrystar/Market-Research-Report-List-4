<p><p>Radio scanners are categorized into different types based on the frequency modulation they support. AM, or Amplitude Modulation, scanners allow users to tune into AM radio frequencies. FM, or Frequency Modulation, scanners enable users to listen to FM radio stations. VFM, or Very High Frequency Modulation, scanners operate on higher frequencies than FM scanners. Additionally, there are other market types of radio scanners that support various other modulation techniques and frequencies, providing a wide range of options for users to choose from.</p></p>
