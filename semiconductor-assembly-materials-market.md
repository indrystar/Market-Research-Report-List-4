<p><p>The semiconductor assembly materials market comprises several key types essential for packaging and protecting semiconductor devices. Die attach adhesives are utilized to bond chips to substrates. Die encapsulants provide protection against environmental factors. Lid seal adhesives secure lids to packages, ensuring moisture resistance. Permanent bonding dielectrics offer electrical insulation and thermal stability. Lastly, thermal interface materials enhance heat dissipation between components. Together, these materials play a critical role in ensuring the reliability and performance of semiconductor devices.</p></p>
