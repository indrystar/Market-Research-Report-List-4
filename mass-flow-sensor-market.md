<p><p>The Mass Flow Sensor market includes various types such as Hot Wire Air Flow Sensor, Hot Film Air Flow Sensor, Karman Vortex Air Flow Sensor, and Vane Air Flow Sensor. </p><p>Hot Wire Air Flow Sensor measures air intake by detecting changes in electrical resistance. Hot Film Air Flow Sensor uses a heated film to measure the air flow. Karman Vortex Air Flow Sensor utilizes vortices shed from a bluff body to calculate flow rate. Vane Air Flow Sensor measures airflow by detecting the pressure difference between two points. Each type offers unique advantages and applications in monitoring and controlling air flow.</p></p>
