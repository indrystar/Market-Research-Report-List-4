<p><p>Turbine oil additives can be classified into two main types: single component additives and additive packages. Single component additives are individual chemicals that are added to turbine oil to enhance specific properties such as oxidation resistance, wear protection, or thermal stability. On the other hand, additive packages are combinations of multiple additives formulated to provide a comprehensive solution for improving overall performance and longevity of turbine oil. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining the efficiency and reliability of turbine systems.</p></p>
