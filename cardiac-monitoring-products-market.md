<p><p>Cardiac monitoring products market includes various types such as resting ECG, stress ECG, Holter ECG, and others. Resting ECG involves recording the heart's electrical activity while the patient is at rest. Stress ECG measures the heart's activity during physical exertion or stress. Holter ECG provides continuous monitoring of the heart's electrical activity over a 24-hour period. Other types of cardiac monitoring products may include event monitors or implantable devices that track the heart's rhythm and detect any irregularities.</p></p>
