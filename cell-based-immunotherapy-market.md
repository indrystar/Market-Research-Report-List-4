<p><p>Cell-based Immunotherapy refers to treatments that utilize the body's own immune cells to target and destroy cancer cells. Autologous Cellular Immunotherapy involves harvesting a patient's own immune cells, modifying them in a lab, and then re-infusing them back into the patient to attack cancer cells. Chimeric Antigen Receptor (CAR) T-Cell Therapy involves genetically modifying T cells to recognize and kill cancer cells. Dendritic Cell-based Vaccine Therapy uses dendritic cells to stimulate the immune system to target and destroy cancer cells.</p></p>
