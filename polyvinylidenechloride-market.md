<p><p>Polyvinylidenechloride (PVDC) market is divided into two main types: PVDC Resins and PVDC Latex. PVDC Resins are solid polymer materials used in packaging applications, providing high barrier properties against oxygen and moisture. On the other hand, PVDC Latex is a water-based dispersion of PVDC resin used as a coating for paper, plastics, and textiles to enhance barrier properties. Both market types play a crucial role in industries such as packaging, textiles, and construction for their excellent barrier properties and durability.</p></p>
