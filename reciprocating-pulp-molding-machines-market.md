<p><p>Reciprocating pulp molding machines come in two main types - automatic and semi-automatic. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some manual intervention for loading/unloading materials and adjusting settings. Both types offer benefits such as high efficiency, precision molding, and cost-effectiveness.
