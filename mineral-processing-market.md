<p><p>Mineral processing market involves various types of operations such as crushing, screening, grinding, and classification. Crushing is the process of reducing the size of the ore particles to liberate the valuable minerals. Screening involves separating particles based on size to ensure efficient processing. Grinding is the process of reducing the size of the particles further through mechanical means. Classification refers to separating particles based on their properties such as size, shape, and density to optimize the processing efficiency. These operations are essential in extracting valuable minerals from the ore.</p></p>
