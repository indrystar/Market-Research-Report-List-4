<p><p>Ferrite core fixed inductors are commonly classified into two main types - the multi-layer type and the wire wound type. Multi-layer type inductors are constructed using multiple layers of ferrite material, allowing for higher inductance values in a compact size. On the other hand, wire wound type inductors are made by winding wire around a ferrite core, providing good efficiency and stable performance. Both types cater to different applications and requirements in various industries, offering a wide range of options for engineers and designers.</p></p>
