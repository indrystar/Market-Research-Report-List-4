<p><strong>The Noise Barriers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reflective type</li><li>Absorptive type</li><li>Mixed type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Noise barriers are categorized into three types in the market: Reflective, Absorptive, and Mixed. Reflective noise barriers are designed to reflect sound waves away and are typically made of hard materials like concrete or metal. Absorptive barriers, on the other hand, are made of softer materials like foam or fiberglass to absorb sound waves. Mixed type barriers combine both reflective and absorptive elements to effectively mitigate noise pollution. Each type offers different benefits and is used in various settings based on the specific noise reduction needs.</p></p>
