<p><p>The automotive circuit breaker market consists of two main types: auto reset and manual reset circuit breakers. Auto reset circuit breakers automatically restore power after a fault is cleared, enhancing convenience and reducing downtime. In contrast, manual reset circuit breakers require human intervention to restore power, providing an added layer of control for safety-critical applications. Both types serve to protect automotive electrical systems from overloads and short circuits, ensuring reliable vehicle performance and safety.</p></p>
