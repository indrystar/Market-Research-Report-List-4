<p><p>Rotational Moulding Equipment market consists of various types such as Rock and Roll Machines, Shuttle Machines, Carousel Machines, Clamshell Machines, and Others. Rock and Roll Machines are designed to rotate the mold in a rocking motion. Shuttle Machines have multiple arms that move the mold between heating and cooling stations. Carousel Machines use a rotating carousel to move the mold through heating, cooling, and molding stages. Clamshell Machines have a two-piece mold that opens and closes like a clamshell. Other types of machines include custom or hybrid designs catering to specific industry needs.</p></p>
