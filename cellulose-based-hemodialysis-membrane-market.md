<p><ul><li>Regenerated Cellulose Membrane</li><li>Modified Cellulose Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cellulose based hemodialysis membrane market includes two main types: regenerated cellulose membrane and modified cellulose membrane. Regenerated cellulose membranes are derived from natural cellulose sources and have high biocompatibility and smooth surface for efficient solute transfer. Modified cellulose membranes are chemically altered to enhance specific properties like improved flux, biocompatibility, and reduced clotting. These membranes are widely used in hemodialysis to remove waste products and excess fluid from the blood of patients with kidney failure.</p></p>
