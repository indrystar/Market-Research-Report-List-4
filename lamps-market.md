<p><p>The lamps market consists of three main types: LED, HID, and halogen. LED (Light Emitting Diode) lamps are energy-efficient, long-lasting, and environmentally friendly, making them increasingly popular for residential and commercial use. HID (High-Intensity Discharge) lamps produce a bright light and are commonly used in outdoor and industrial applications due to their high lumen output. Halogen lamps, a type of incandescent lighting, offer better efficiency and a whiter light but have a shorter lifespan compared to LEDs. Each type caters to distinct lighting needs and preferences.</p></p>
