PCR-based and microarray techniques involve amplifying DNA samples while assay-based techniques detect antibodies in blood. Massively parallel sequencing allows for rapid and accurate analysis of multiple blood group genes simultaneously. These technologies play a crucial role in blood transfusions, organ transplants, and paternity testing, ensuring compatibility and reducing the risk of adverse reactions. Continued advancements in these technologies are expected to further enhance the accuracy and efficiency of blood group typing.</p></p>
